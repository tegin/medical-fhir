===============================
Medical Administration Location
===============================

Extension of medical that adds a Medical Administration Center entity as
parents of Locations.

Usage
=====

#. Go to 'Medical / Administration / Center'
#. Click 'Create' and fill in the information.
#. Click 'Save'
